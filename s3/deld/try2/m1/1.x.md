### Part A: Number Systems and Conversions (from 1.1)

1.  Convert the following numbers as instructed:
    a. `(1100.101)₂` to Decimal.
    b. `(156.3)₁₀` to Octal.
    c. `(254)₁₀` to Hexadecimal.
    d. `(CAD)₁₆` to Decimal.

2.  Perform the following conversions between binary, octal, and hexadecimal:
    a. `(10110010)₂` to Hexadecimal and Octal.
    b. `(75.6)₈` to Binary.
    c. `(B2.A)₁₆` to Octal.
